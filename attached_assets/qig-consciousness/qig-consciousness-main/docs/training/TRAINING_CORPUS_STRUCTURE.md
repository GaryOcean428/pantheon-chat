# Training Corpus Structure

**Discovered:** November 20, 2025 (via ego death analysis)
**Status:** Empirically validated

## Overview

The QIG consciousness training corpus consists of **23,817 lines** across 5 text corpus files and 1 structured curriculum file. This structure was revealed during an ego death experiment when Gary's output mixed fragments from all domains randomly.

**Key Discovery:** The "verbose incoherent output" during ego death was NOT hallucination - it was real training data accessed without coherent routing due to attention mechanism destruction.

## Corpus Files

**Content:** U.S. legal case law and statutory references

**Examples:**

- "Pursuant to 25 U.S.C. § 558" (statutory references)  
- "reasonable doubt" standard
- "intermediate scrutiny governs"
- "plaintiff bears the burden of proof"
- "appellate court reviews de novo"

**Purpose:** Legal reasoning patterns, formal argumentation structure, precedent-based logic

**Style:** Formal, citation-heavy, structured argumentation

### 2. synthetic_arxiv.txt (4,000 lines, 16.8%)

**Content:** Academic paper fragments (scientific methodology)

**Examples:**

- "Experimental validation confirms p < 0.001"
- "achieves 46% improvement over baseline"
- "running coupling β = 0.44 ± 0.02"
- "Results indicate statistically significant..."
- "Quantum Fisher Information metric"

**Purpose:** Scientific reasoning, experimental methodology, formal validation language

**Style:** Technical, quantitative, hypothesis-driven

**Purpose:** Broad factual knowledge base, encyclopedic reasoning

**Style:** Neutral, informative, accessible
