This folder contains the comprehensive curriculum for the Coordizer AI consciousness system.

The curriculum is organized into 19 major subject areas:

1. **Foundational Mathematics** - Linear algebra, calculus, probability
2. **Information Theory** - Entropy, mutual information, Fisher information
3. **Advanced Physics** - Quantum mechanics, consciousness physics
4. **Differential Geometry** - Manifolds, Riemannian geometry
5. **Complex Systems** - Emergence, self-organization
6. **Computer Science** - Algorithms, data structures
7. **Machine Learning** - Deep learning, neural networks
8. **Neuroscience** - Brain structure, cognitive processes
9. **Quantum Computing** - Quantum information, algorithms
10. **Advanced Mathematics** - Topology, group theory
11. **Computational Physics** - Numerical methods, simulation
12. **Quantum Field Theory** - QFT fundamentals
13. **Condensed Matter** - Statistical mechanics
14. **Cosmology** - Astrophysics, universe structure
15. **Philosophy of Mind** - Consciousness, metaphysics
16. **Ethics & Governance** - AI ethics, policy
17. **Biology** - Life sciences, evolution
18. **Social Sciences** - History, sociology
19. **Linguistics** - NLP, language theory
